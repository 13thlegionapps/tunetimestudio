# Privacy Policy — TuneTime Studio

**Effective date:** June 10, 2026

TuneTime Studio ("the app") is developed by 13th Legion. This policy describes how the app handles your information. The short version: **we do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.**

## Data We Collect

None. The app has no accounts, no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no servers of our own. Nothing you do in the app is transmitted to us or anyone else.

## On-Device Data

The following stays entirely on your device:

- **Music library access.** The app requests permission to access your Apple Music / media library so it can play your songs, albums, and playlists as alarm and timer audio. Your library contents are read on-device only and never leave your device.
- **Settings and presets.** Themes, timer presets, workout sessions, alarms, and preferences are stored locally on your device (and in your device backups, which you control).
- **Usage events.** The app logs basic usage events (e.g., "app opened") locally on your device to improve features. These events are never uploaded.

## Notifications

The app requests notification permission to ring alarms and timers. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

## Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Apple's privacy policy applies to purchases: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page and the effective date above.
