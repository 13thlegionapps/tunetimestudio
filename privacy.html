Privacy Policy

TuneTime Studio  ·  13th Legion, LLC  ·  Last updated: June 2026

TuneTime Studio is an iOS music timer, alarm, and ambient sound app published by 13th Legion, LLC. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and what we do with it — which is nothing, because the app operates entirely on your device.

No Data Collection

TuneTime Studio does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no accounts, no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no servers of our own. Nothing you do in the app is transmitted to us or anyone else.

Music Library Access

TuneTime Studio requests permission to access your Apple Music / media library solely to play your songs, albums, and playlists as alarm and timer audio. Your library is read on-device only and its contents never leave your device.

Settings and Presets

Your themes, timer presets, workout sessions, alarms, and preferences are stored locally on your device (and in your device backups, which you control). This data is never shared.

Usage Events

The app logs basic usage events (such as "app opened") locally on your device to improve features. These events are never uploaded.

Notifications

The app requests notification permission to ring alarms and timers. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Apple's privacy policy applies to purchases: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Children's Privacy

TuneTime Studio does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of the app, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at tunetimesupport@13th-legion.com.
